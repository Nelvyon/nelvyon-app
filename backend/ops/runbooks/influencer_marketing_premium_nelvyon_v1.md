# NELVYON Premium OS Standards — Influencer Marketing v1

**Parent:** [`nelvyon_master_operations_v1.md`](./nelvyon_master_operations_v1.md)

## NAME

Influencer Marketing Premium OS Standards for NELVYON v1 (client delivery checklist — **paperwork layer only**)

## Purpose

This **Influencer Marketing Premium** template is **OS-facing delivery paperwork**: statuses, evidence, **type badges** (nano, micro, macro, mega, brand_ambassador, ugc_creator, celebrity, b2b_thought_leader), and links — it does **not** connect influencer marketplaces, talent agencies APIs, or e-signature for live contracts from this surface.

Suggested touchpoints for honest verification:

- `/os/social-media-premium/preview` — when publishing cadence and channel rules overlap paperwork  
- `/os/contenido-copywriting-premium/preview` — when scripts, captions, and disclosures align with copy governance  
- `/os/ads-premium/preview` — when paid boosting or whitelisting overlaps media plan paperwork  
- `/help` — FTC/ASA-style disclosure, IP, or talent payment escalations  

---

## Scope (minimum real)

- **Delivery discipline:** KPIs, audience fit, exclusivity, and usage rights documented before client sign-off.
- **Honesty:** Follower tiers (nano→mega) and fraud checks described — no “guaranteed viral” claims from OS alone.
- **Legal:** #ad disclosure, music rights, and whitelisting windows explicit — template does not generate binding contracts.

---

## Limits (explicit non-goals)

- No AspireIQ/GRIN/Upfluence-style marketplace APIs from this OS template.
- No creation or e-sign of real talent agreements from this preview.
- Does not modify closed product fronts; does not open `/crm/deals`.

---

## Premium checklist (influencer marketing delivery)

| Area | Premium expectation |
| --- | --- |
| READY | Golden path / `pnpm gate` discipline when influencer-related surfaces ship in the same release train. |
| STRATEGY & OBJECTIVES | KPI mix, budget split, and brand safety tier documented. |
| SEARCH & SELECTION | Vetting, audience overlap, and fraud signals explicit — no scraping of private DMs from OS. |
| BRIEFING & CONTRACT | Deliverables, usage rights, kill fees, and disclosure rules honest — execution in legal tooling outside NELVYON. |
| CONTENT PRODUCTION | Shot lists, approvals, and reshoot policy explicit — no raw asset hosting from template. |
| PUBLICATION & TRACKING | Posting windows, whitelisting, and UTM discipline described — no auto-publish from OS. |
| METRICS & ROI | EMV, CPA, lift definitions limited to agreed methodology — no platform API pulls from checklist. |
| FINAL REPORTING | Learnings, renewals, and caveats explicit — template records expectations only. |
