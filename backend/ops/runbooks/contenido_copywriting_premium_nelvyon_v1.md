## NAME

Contenido y Copywriting Premium OS Standards for NELVYON v1 (client delivery checklist — **paperwork layer only**)

## Purpose

This **Contenido Copywriting Premium** template is **OS-facing delivery paperwork**: statuses, evidence, **format badges** (blog, landing, web copy, email copy, ads copy, guión, redes sociales, SEO content), and links — it does **not** connect LLM/content APIs, CMS publish hooks, or automated generation pipelines.

Suggested workspace / OS touchpoints for honest verification:

- `/app/branding` — tone and visual guardrails when copy ships with brand policy  
- `/help` — legal/compliance escalation when claims or regulated verticals appear  
- `/os/seo-premium/preview`, `/os/web-premium/preview`, `/os/ads-premium/preview` — when delivery story overlaps SEO, web, or ads templates (read-only framing)  
- `/os/social-media-premium/preview`, `/os/email-marketing-premium/preview` — cross-check when social or email copy packs are in scope  

---

## Scope (minimum real)

- **Delivery discipline:** Format matrix and voice pillars documented before client sign-off.
- **Editorial hygiene:** Calendar, revision rounds, and quality bar explicit — no ghost “unlimited revisions”.
- **SEO honesty:** On-page recommendations framed as checklist items, not live rank guarantees.

---

## Limits (explicit non-goals)

- No OpenAI/Anthropic/Gemini or other generative content APIs from this template.
- Does not modify closed product fronts; does not open `/crm/deals`.
- No scraping competitor copy or automated plagiarism checks from this OS surface.

| Area | Premium expectation |
| --- | --- |
| READY | Golden path / `pnpm gate` discipline when content-related web surfaces ship in the same release train. |

| DELIVERABLES & REPORTING | Pack list (docs, exports) reconciled; metrics claims limited to agreed definitions. |
