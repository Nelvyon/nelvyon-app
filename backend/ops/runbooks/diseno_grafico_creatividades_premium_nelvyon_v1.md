**Parent:** [`nelvyon_master_operations_v1.md`](./nelvyon_master_operations_v1.md)

## NAME

Diseño gráfico y creatividades Premium OS Standards for NELVYON v1 (client delivery checklist — **paperwork layer only**)

## Purpose

This **Diseño gráfico Premium** template is **OS-facing delivery paperwork**: statuses, evidence, **format badges** (banner_digital, flyer, cartel, infografia, presentacion, packaging, creatividad_ads, post_social, kit_brand), and links — it does **not** connect Figma/Canva/Adobe APIs, asset CDNs, or generative design services.

Suggested OS / workspace touchpoints for honest verification:

- `/os/branding-premium/preview` — when identity systems and kit_brand overlap  
- `/os/social-media-premium/preview` — when post_social crops and calendars bind  
- `/os/ads-premium/preview` — when creatividad_ads specs and policy copy align  
- `/os/contenido-copywriting-premium/preview` — when headlines and legal disclaimers ship with layouts  
- `/help` — IP, font licensing, or marketplace creative policy escalations  

---

## Scope (minimum real)

- **Delivery discipline:** Brief, audience, channels, and revision rounds documented before client sign-off.
- **Asset hygiene:** Source files vs export naming, bleed/safe zones, and color profile (e.g. print vs digital) explicit — no silent “unlimited rounds”.
- **Honesty:** Template records agreed formats only — no automated asset generation or rights clearance from this surface.

---

## Limits (explicit non-goals)

- No Figma/Adobe/Canva or similar design SaaS APIs from this template.
- Does not modify closed product fronts; does not open `/crm/deals`.
- No AI “instant campaign pack” or stock clearance guarantees from this OS surface alone.

---

## Premium checklist (graphic design delivery)

| Area | Premium expectation |
| --- | --- |
| READY | Golden path / `pnpm gate` discipline when design-related web surfaces ship in the same release train. |
| BRIEF & CONCEPT | Goals, audience, tone, references, and success criteria documented — aligned with brand where applicable. |
| SKETCHES & PROPOSALS | Direction count, rationale, and client pick workflow explicit. |
| DESIGN & COMPOSITION | Grid, typography, hierarchy, and accessibility contrast notes honest. |
| REVIEW & FEEDBACK | Consolidated feedback, version naming, and scope change rules explicit. |
| ADAPTATIONS & FORMATS | Master → derivative sizes per channel; no phantom “auto-resize all networks”. |
| DELIVERY | Handoff package (PDF/PNG/SVG/etc.), folder structure, and checksums — storage outside NELVYON unless product says otherwise. |
| REPORTING | Performance claims limited to agreed definitions — template records expectations only. |
