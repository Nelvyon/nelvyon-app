## NAME

Reputación online y ORM Premium OS Standards for NELVYON v1 (client delivery checklist — **paperwork layer only**)

## Purpose

This **ORM / reputación Premium** template is **OS-facing delivery paperwork**: statuses, evidence, **type badges** (auditoria_reputacion, gestion_resenas, contenido_positivo, supresion_negativo, monitorizacion_marca, crisis_management, reporting), and links — it does **not** connect review platforms, brand monitoring SaaS, or scraping APIs from this surface.

Suggested touchpoints for honest verification:

- `/os/seo-premium/preview` — when SERP and technical crawl hygiene overlap ORM paperwork  
- `/os/contenido-copywriting-premium/preview` — when positive content and messaging align with copy governance  
- `/help` — defamation, platform ToS, or legal escalation paths  

---

## Scope (minimum real)

- **Delivery discipline:** Baseline sentiment map, channel list, and escalation tree documented before client sign-off.
- **Honesty:** “Removal” or suppression claims limited to lawful, platform-specific processes — no guaranteed delisting from OS alone.
- **Monitoring:** Cadence and tool ownership described — template does not poll external APIs.

---

## Limits (explicit non-goals)

- No third-party reputation, review, or social listening API connections from this OS template.
- No scraping of reviews, maps, or social feeds from this preview.
- Does not modify closed product fronts; does not open `/crm/deals`.

| Area | Premium expectation |

| MONTHLY REPORTING | Metrics definitions and limitations explicit — template records expectations only. |
