# NELVYON Premium OS Standards — Social Media v1

**Parent:** [`nelvyon_master_operations_v1.md`](./nelvyon_master_operations_v1.md)

## NAME

Social Media Premium OS Standards for NELVYON v1 (client delivery checklist for managed social programs — **paperwork layer only**)

## Purpose

This **Social Media Premium** template is **OS-facing delivery paperwork**: statuses, evidence, platform badges, and links — it does **not** connect Meta/TikTok/X/LinkedIn/YouTube APIs, schedulers, or listening tools.

Suggested workspace touchpoints for honest verification (no new social infra):

- `/app/branding` — visual guardrails that often pair with social creative packs  
- `/app/communications` — owned channel posture when cross-posting or email bridges apply  
- `/campaigns` — when social pilots tie to named workspace campaigns  
- `/help` — escalation when moderation or legal exceeds product scope  

---

## Scope (minimum real)

- **Delivery discipline:** Platform matrix (Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X/Twitter, Facebook, YouTube) reconciled with what ops can actually execute manually.
- **Editorial hygiene:** Strategy, calendar, creative/copy, publishing rhythm, and community rules documented before client sign-off.
- **Metrics honesty:** Reach/growth claims limited to agreed definitions — template records expectations only.

---

## Limits (explicit non-goals)

- No OAuth, Graph API, TikTok Business API, or third-party social SaaS wiring from this template.
- Does not modify closed product fronts; does not open `/crm/deals`.
- No automated posting, scraping, or sentiment scoring from this OS surface.
