# NELVYON v1.1 — Product One-Pager (Client/Investor)

NELVYON is a workspace-scoped operations platform designed for teams that need real self-service execution, clear permissions, and visible trust signals.

## What NELVYON v1.1 does today

- Self-activation and guided onboarding in-product.
- Role-based workspace operation with explicit permission boundaries.
- Built-in help center + first-line bot with honest handoff behavior.
- Operations dashboard with actionable health and queue signals.
- Billing visibility (plan, usage, invoices) with transparent limit messaging.
- Real self-service commercial flow: plan/cycle selection, checkout, verification, and reflected active subscription state.
- Workspace audit/security visibility with recent activity and filtered event history.

## Why this matters

- Reduces dependence on manual support for day-to-day operation.
- Gives enterprise buyers concrete confidence signals (traceability + role controls).
- Converts commercial intent directly in-product through real upgrade journey.

## Trust and scope principles

- No fake states: UI reflects backend truth.
- Workspace isolation across sensitive flows.
- Minimal but practical audit visibility for operational confidence.
- Lightweight telemetry constrained by explicit no-PII/no-secrets policy.

## Current scope boundaries (to avoid overpromise)

- Not a full SIEM/SOC or full GRC suite.
- Not full OIDC enterprise rollout yet in the frontend user journey.
- Advanced subscription lifecycle controls (e.g., deep downgrade/cycle orchestration) are roadmap, not v1.1 core.

## v1.1 commercial positioning

"NELVYON v1.1 is sellable now for teams that need credible self-service operations and billing flows with clear permission and audit posture."
